About This Course

Our Korean Language program is specifically designed for candidates preparing for the EPS-TOPIK (Employment Permit System - Test of Proficiency in Korean). We focus on the official HRD Korea curriculum to ensure maximum exam success.

The course covers intensive vocabulary building, grammar patterns common in the EPS exam, and extensive listening practice using official materials.

Course Curriculum

Module 1: Hangul & Basic Foundations
  • Reading and writing Hangul characters
  • Pronunciation rules and Batchim
  • Basic sentence structure (Subject-Object-Verb)
  • Essential daily greetings
Module 2: EPS TOPIK Vocabulary & Grammar
  • Industry-specific vocabulary (Manufacturing, Agriculture)
  • Formal and informal honorifics
  • Complex verb endings and particles
  • Time, dates, and counting systems
Module 3: Mock Exams & Strategy
  • Speed-reading for the RBT (Reading Based Test)
  • Listening strategies for LBT (Listening Based Test)
  • Analyzing previous EPS TOPIK question papers
  • Personalized feedback on performance
